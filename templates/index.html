On-Device AI for ASU — the 8 Empower 2026 use cases

Eight AI experiences mapped directly to ASU's published priorities, plus a Hybrid AI / Microsoft 365 mock-up — all running locally on a Copilot+ PC NPU via Foundry Local. Nothing leaves the device.

  • Practice AI — quiz yourself on key concepts.
  • Assignment Coach — break projects into milestones.
  • Writing Guide — structured pre-submit feedback.

🧑‍💼 For Staff

  • Onboarding — 30/60/90 ramp-up for new hires.

  • Dev Coach — goals, reflection, growth.
